Bio

Christian Z. Ashley is a digital artist from Upstate New York (born in 1993). He has a particular affinity for character design and animation as well as the multitude of ways people create characters out of themselves both on- and offline. He uses modes such as Photoshop for traditional design and screen-capture for recording digital interaction. Christian is currently a student at Clarkson University in Potsdam New York and will be graduating with his BS in Digital Arts & Sciences in December 2016.
